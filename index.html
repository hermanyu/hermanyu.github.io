About Me

Professional data sciencist working in marketing effectiveness analytics with an educational background in mathematics. My passion projects are in recommender systems including content-based recommendation using image recognition. Currently building a fullstack web application to deploy interactive recommender models.

What I Do

Professionally, I build Media Mix Models (MMMs) to attribute sales to marketing spend. This allows our clients to accurately measure ROI in their marketing investments and optimize marketing strategies. MMMs use econometric modeling and statistical inference to circumvent the issues of multi-touch tracking (or lack-thereof). I explain MMMs in more detail here.

Recommender systems are my primary field of interst, which can be viewed as "dual" to the concept of marketing. Where as image generation and large-language models, attempt to solve very awe-inspiring problems, recommender systems attempt solve very relatable ones. How often have you been stuck on the question "what should we do for dinner?" Recommender systems present a "blue collar" side of machine learning, yet the engineering challenges are nuanced and complex.
